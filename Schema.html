Pradhan Mantri Fasal Bima Yojana is the government sponsored crop insurance scheme that integrates multiple stakeholders on a single platform.

Soil Health Card

A Soil Health Card is used to assess the current status of soil health and, when used over time, to determine changes in soil health that are affected by land management.

Agmarket

It is a govt. portal on agricultural marketing backed by a wide area information network connecting agricultural markets and also providing linkages to the important Organisations.
